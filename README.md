---

## 📌 About the Project

FloodGuard Kisan is a digital platform designed to support farmers living in flood-prone regions. During the monsoon season, sudden floods often destroy crops, damage farmland, and cause heavy financial losses.

This project provides early flood alerts and simple farming guidance so that farmers can take preventive steps before flooding impacts their crops.

---

## 🌾 Problem

Flooding is one of the major challenges faced by farmers in many parts of India. In several regions, farmers do not receive timely warnings or reliable information that could help them prepare in advance.

Because of this gap, crops are damaged every year and farmers suffer financial losses.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

FloodGuard Kisan provides a mobile platform that delivers early alerts and practical guidance for farmers.

The system collects weather and river data, analyzes flood risk, and sends notifications to farmers so they can take preventive action before floods occur.

---

## 🏗 System Workflow

Weather Data → Flood Risk Analysis → Alert Generation → Mobile Notification → Farmer Action
